About Ted

Ted Bordeau is a Kansas City native currently based in LA. He began playing the piano at age five, teaching himself classics by Billy Joel, Elton John, and the Beatles. By age 12, Ted was already running the local bar circuit as a drummer in the folk rock band Casey’s Train. At age 14, he developed a solo act, playing and singing piano bar tunes around Kansas City. Today, Ted splits his time between Los Angeles, where he is studying songwriting at USC’s prestigious Thornton School of Music, and Kansas City, where he fronts his own classic rock band Joyride.
